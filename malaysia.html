Why Study in Malaysia?

Malaysia is emerging as a popular study-abroad destination for international students, particularly from India. The country offers high-quality education at relatively affordable costs, making it an attractive option for students seeking a global education. Many Malaysian universities rank highly in Asia and globally, offering a dynamic and multicultural student life. The Malaysian Ministry of Higher Education actively promotes foreign student enrollment and offers various scholarships and financial aid to ease the transition for international students.

Know why Malaysia is so popular to Bangladeshi students

Malaysia is a top destination for Bangladeshi students, offering high-quality education at affordable costs and incredible scholarship opportunities. With the Malaysian government investing heavily in international education, students can access globally recognized degrees in a multicultural and welcoming environment. This, combined with modern facilities and a student-friendly atmosphere, makes Malaysia an excellent choice for higher studies.

Admission Requirements for Malaysia Study

Here are the major requirements to study in MA which you need to ensure while applying to a MA university:

  • Copy of a minimum 18 months valid passport
  • Academic Transcripts and Certificate from the previous study (O-level/A-Level, SSC/HSC/Diploma, GED, Bachelors/Masters)
  • Work Experience letter (for Postgraduation)
  • Proof of English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS / PTE)
  • Statement of Purpose (SOP)
  • Essays (if demanded by the university)
  • CV/Resume
