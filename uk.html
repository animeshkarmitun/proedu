Why should you choose United Kingdom?

Studying in the UK provides world-class education at top-ranked universities, offering internationally recognized degrees valued by employers. With diverse courses in traditional and emerging fields, the UK excels in research, enabling students to work with renowned experts. It also offers a safe, modern, and student-friendly environment with excellent healthcare, transport, and accommodation.

Know why United Kingdom is so popular to Bangladeshi students

The UK is a top choice for Bangladeshi students due to its world-class education, diverse courses, and post-study work opportunities. A significant Bangladeshi diaspora and historical ties create a sense of familiarity, while studying in the UK enhances global connections and career growth.

Admission Requirements for United Kingdom Study Abroad

Here are the major requirements to study in UK which you need to ensure while applying to a UK university:

  • Copy of a minimum 18 months valid passport
  • Academic Transcripts and Certificate from the previous study (O-level/A-Level, SSC/HSC/Diploma, GED, Bachelors/Masters)
  • Work Experience letter (for Postgraduation)
  • Proof of English proficiency (TOEFL/IELTS / PTE / DOULINGO / OIETC)
  • Statement of Purpose (SOP) & Recommendation letter (LOR)
  • Gap Explanation (if demanded by the university)
  • CV/Resume
